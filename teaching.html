Teaching

My approach to music education features a blend of fundamentals, technique, and repertoire, with emphasis on theory. I first assess where the student's current understanding and abilities, then devise a plan to build and reinforce her/his musical foundation.

A central feature of my teaching is tailoring lessons to the student, drawing insights and inspiration from each individual. Existing educational literature provides some appropriate lessons and exercises, but I also design brand new material for each particular student. This allows our work to stay focused on what the student is ready to learn, rather than following a one-size-fits-all prescription.

I delight in seeing the [dawning?] light of new understanding brighten people's eyes. Whatever the musical goals of my students, I work not only to equip them with tools to reach those goals, but also to deepen their fundamental experience of music.
